Coronavirus Originated From Wuhan Lab, Claims Trump; Says 'WHO Acts As PR Agency Of China'

New Delhi: The US President Donald Trump has accused the World Health Organisation (WHO) of being a public relations agency for China. According to a report by PTI, Trump said on Thursday that the World Health Organization should be "ashamed of" itself and that the organsiation is acting as a public relations agency for China.

The Trump administration has also launched a probe to look at the role of WHO in the coronavirus pandemic and has suspended financial assistance to it.

"I think that the World Health Organization should be ashamed of themselves because they are like the public relations agency for China," Trump told reporters in the East Room of the White House.

The reports say that although he did not hold the President of the People's Republic of China, Xi Jinping responsible for the pandemic he said that a lot more could have done to stop the pandemic.

"I don't want to say that, I don't want to say that, but certainly it could have been stopped. It came out of China and it could have been stopped and I wish they had stopped it and so does the whole world wish they had stopped it."
